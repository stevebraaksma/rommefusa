DR ROBERT MITCHELL MEMORIAL FOUNDATION INC. (RoMMeF)

The Dr Robert Mitchell Memorial Foundation (RoMMeF) is a non-governmental, non-profit, organization established in 2008 to help children with cancer in Ghana gain access to affordable and quality health care.

The foundation was established in memory of the late son of Mrs. Emma Mitchell, Dr. Robert Mitchell, who passed away while in medical training in the United States.

RoMMeF USA was established to raise funds from Ghanians and other supporters abroad to send to assist the work of RoMMeF in Ghana. To learn more about the work of RoMMeF in Ghana you may visit https://therommef.org/
